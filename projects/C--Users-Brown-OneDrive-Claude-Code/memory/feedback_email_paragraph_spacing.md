---
name: Email formatting requirements (dashes and spacing)
description: All emails must be free of dashes of any kind and must have proper paragraph spacing with hard returns between paragraphs.
type: feedback
---

Two standing requirements for ALL outgoing emails:

1. **No dashes of any kind.** No em dashes, en dashes, double dashes (--), or single hyphens used as dashes. Use periods, commas, or rewrite the sentence instead. Compound-word hyphens (e.g., "follow-up") are fine.

2. **Proper paragraph spacing.** Every paragraph must be separated by a blank line with hard returns. No wall-of-text formatting.

**Why:** Josh flagged both as standing requirements during HITL review on 2026-04-07. Dashes look unprofessional in his emails, and missing paragraph breaks hurt readability.

**How to apply:** When reviewing or drafting any email (HITL tasks, Gmail drafts, replies), check for both issues. When submitting HITL responses, flag "approve with edits" if either issue is present. These apply to all emails, not just specific contacts or campaigns.
