# Brain Bridge Case Studies and Proof Points

Reference material for selecting relevant social proof when drafting outreach. Match case studies to the prospect's industry, company size, or pain point.

---

## Case Studies (Anonymized, Verified)

### 1. Digital Marketing Agency (15-25 employees)
- **Deployed**: AI Teammate for campaign management
- **Result**: Reduced routine client communication time by ~30%
- **Key insight**: Creative agencies respond well when AI handles operational burden
- **Best for**: Marketing, creative services, agencies

### 2. Tech CEO Email Assistant (3D scanning startup)
- **Deployed**: Personal AI email assistant
- **Result**: Saves 20-30 min/day
- **Key insight**: Highest-ROI deployment can be the simplest. Single AI Teammate for a single high-value person.
- **Best for**: Founders, CEOs, solo execs overwhelmed by email

### 3. Education Pathway Mapping (Regional science institute)
- **Deployed**: Interactive career/education pathway web application for emerging energy sector
- **Result**: Complex data made navigable
- **Key insight**: AI doesn't always mean chatbots. Sometimes highest value is making complex data accessible.
- **Best for**: Education, workforce development, nonprofits

### 4. National Staffing Company (Commercial drivers)
- **Deployed**: 3 AI Teammates: Compliance Specialist, Driver Experience Teammate (voice-based), Carrier Development Teammate. Full Microsoft Teams integration.
- **Result**: Compliance overhead significantly reduced
- **Key insight**: Staffing industry has massive compliance overhead AI can automate.
- **Best for**: Staffing, HR, compliance-heavy industries

### 5. Small Business AI Support (Optics/photonics sector org)
- **Deployed**: Ecosystem mapping of 100+ businesses, AI adoption workshops, provider onboarding framework, podcast series
- **Result**: Replicable sector-wide AI adoption program
- **Key insight**: Replicable model for sector-specific AI adoption programs.
- **Best for**: Industry associations, economic development, sector orgs

### 6. Large School District (~44 HR professionals)
- **Deployed**: AI readiness assessment, specialized HR AI workshop, hands-on training building custom AI assistants for HR tasks
- **Result**: HR teams went from afraid of AI to creatively engaged
- **Key insight**: HR teams go from afraid to creative when given hands-on experience.
- **Best for**: Education, K-12 districts, HR departments, government

### 7. National Advertising Association (Local chapter)
- **Deployed**: AI workshop tailored to advertising professionals for campaign management, content creation, client services
- **Best for**: Professional associations, advertising, events

## Matching Rules

When selecting a case study for outreach, match on at least 2 of these 3:
1. **Industry**: Same or adjacent industry as the prospect
2. **Role**: Similar decision-maker level (CEO, VP Ops, HR Director, etc.)
3. **Pain point**: Similar operational challenge

If no case study matches on 2 of 3, use key metrics/proof points instead of forcing a weak case study match.
