# Qualification Frameworks

Two frameworks used in the Brain Bridge GTM qualification pipeline. Apply them in sequence as deals progress through the pipeline.

---

## F.L.O.O.R. — Cold Outreach Qualification

Best for early-stage qualification during cold outreach. Use at 40% and 60% pipeline stages.

| Letter | Dimension | Question to Answer |
|--------|-----------|--------------------|
| F | Friction | What's broken or blocked right now? |
| L | Line of Decision | Who makes the final call? |
| O | Ownership | Who owns execution day-to-day? |
| O | Ops Bandwidth | Does the team have capacity to act? |
| R | Reason to Act Now | Why is this urgent today, not next quarter? |

**When to use:** Initial cold outreach through early discovery. Prioritizes pain, ownership, and urgency over budget conversations, which are premature at this stage.

**Goal at 40%:** Confirm Ownership (the prospect or someone they control will execute).

**Goal at 60%:** Confirm both Ownership dimensions — the prospect owns execution AND has the operational bandwidth to move forward.

---

## B.A.N.T. — Post-Qualification

Best for mid-to-late pipeline after the prospect has demonstrated interest and intent. Use at 100% pipeline stage.

| Letter | Dimension | Question to Answer |
|--------|-----------|--------------------|
| B | Budget | Is budget allocated or approvable? |
| A | Authority | Can this person make or strongly influence the decision? |
| N | Need | Is there a documented, real need — not hypothetical? |
| T | Timeline | When does this need to be solved? |

**When to use:** After a discovery call confirms the prospect is a real opportunity. Budget conversations are appropriate here because trust and interest have been established.

**Goal at 100%:** All four B.A.N.T. criteria confirmed before advancing to proposal or close.

---

## Default Progression

| Stage | Framework | Hard Gate |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| 40% (Initial Qualification) | F.L.O.O.R. | Prospect must own execution — not just influence |
| 60% (Deeper Qualification) | F.L.O.O.R. | Both Ownership (O) and Ops Bandwidth (O) confirmed |
| 100% (Full Qualification) | B.A.N.T. | All four B.A.N.T. criteria confirmed |

**Hard Gate rule:** A deal cannot advance past a stage gate without the gate criteria being confirmed. Partial confirmation does not qualify.

---

## Campaign-Specific Overrides

The default progression applies to all campaigns unless Section 4 of the Campaign Plan specifies overrides. Common override scenarios:

- **Inbound lead:** May skip 40% gate and enter at 60%
- **Existing relationship:** May enter at 60% or 100% depending on prior qualification
- **Enterprise deal:** May add additional gates (e.g., Legal review, Security review) between 60% and 100%

Document overrides in Section 4 of the Campaign Plan. Do not apply overrides that are not explicitly recorded in the plan.
